global warming is the increase in Earth's temperature due to human activities.it is caused by greenhouse gases like carbon dioxide and methane from burning fossil fuels.


 deforestation worsens the problem by reducing carbon absorption. melting ice caps lead to rising sea levels, threatening coastal areas. ecosystems are disrupted, forcing animals to migrate or face extinction. extreme weather events like hurricanes and heatwaves are becoming more frequent. agriculture is affected, reducing food production. diseases carried by insects like mosquitoes are spreading due to warmer climates. coral reefs are dying because of ocean warming. individuals can help by reducing waste, conserving energy, and supporting sustainable practices. governments and organizations must promote renewable energy and reforestation. global cooperation and innovation are essential for a sustainable future.
